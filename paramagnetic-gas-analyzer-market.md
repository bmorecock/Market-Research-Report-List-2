<p><p>Paramagnetic Gas Analyzers are used to measure the concentration of gases in a sample by detecting the magnetic properties of the gas molecules. The three main types of Paramagnetic Gas Analyzers are Thermal Conductivity Type, Thermal Magnetic Type, and Electrochemistry Type. </p><p>Thermal Conductivity Type analyzers measure the change in heat transfer caused by the presence of gas molecules. Thermal Magnetic Type analyzers measure the magnetic properties of the gas molecules. Electrochemistry Type analyzers use electrodes to detect the presence of gas molecules. Each type has its own advantages and limitations for different applications.</p></p>
