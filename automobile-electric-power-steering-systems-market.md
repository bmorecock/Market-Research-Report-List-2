<p><strong>The Automobile Electric Power Steering Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CEPS</li><li>PEPS</li><li>REPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automobile Electric Power Steering Systems come in three main types: Column Electric Power Steering (CEPS), Pinion Electric Power Steering (PEPS), and Rack Electric Power Steering (REPS). CEPS uses a motor mounted on the steering column to assist with steering, while PEPS has the motor attached to the steering rack. REPS integrates the motor directly into the steering rack for more efficient power steering. Each type offers unique benefits and features tailored to specific vehicle models and driving preferences.</p></p>
