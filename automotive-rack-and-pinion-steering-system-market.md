<p><p>Automotive rack and pinion steering system is a type of steering mechanism commonly used in vehicles to convert rotational motion from the steering wheel into linear motion. This system consists of a pinion gear attached to the steering shaft and a rack that moves side to side to steer the vehicle. It is known for its simplicity, efficiency, and better steering response compared to other steering systems.</p><p>The Automotive Rack and Pinion Steering System Market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing demand for passenger cars and commercial vehicles across the globe.
