<p><strong>The Wing Anti-icing System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical De-icing System</li><li>Electric Pulse Anti-icing System</li><li>Electric Heating Anti-icing System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wing anti-icing systems are crucial in preventing ice buildup on aircraft wings to ensure safe flying conditions. There are three main types of wing anti-icing systems: Mechanical de-icing systems use pneumatic boots to break and shed ice, electric pulse anti-icing systems emit electrical pulses to dislodge ice, and electric heating anti-icing systems use electric elements to melt ice. Each system has its own advantages and applications, catering to different aircraft and operating conditions.</p></p>
