<p><p>Powder-based needle-free injectors come in various types such as jet, spring, laser, and vibration. Jet injectors use high pressure to propel the medication through the skin. Spring injectors use a mechanical spring to deliver the medication. Laser injectors use laser technology to create micropores in the skin for drug delivery. Vibration injectors use vibrations to push the medication through the skin. Each type offers unique advantages and may be preferred based on specific patient needs and preferences.</p></p>
