<p><p>The DTV IC market encompasses various types of chips that are crucial for the functioning of digital television devices. Signal demodulator chips are responsible for decoding and processing incoming signals. Video-control chips manage various aspects of the displayed video, such as resolution and color. MPEG-2 decode chips decode video streams compressed in MPEG-2 format. Video processing chips enhance the quality of video output. Other chips in the market may include audio processors, HDMI controllers, and power management chips, among others.</p></p>
