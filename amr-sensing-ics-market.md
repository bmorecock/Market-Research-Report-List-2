<p><p>AMR Sensing ICs are categorized into two main types based on their output signal: Digital Output AMR Sensing ICs and Analog Output AMR Sensing ICs. Digital Output AMR Sensing ICs provide a binary output signal (1 or 0) based on the presence or absence of a magnetic field, making them suitable for applications requiring simple on/off detection. Analog Output AMR Sensing ICs provide a continuous output signal proportional to the intensity of the magnetic field, offering higher resolution and accuracy for applications requiring precise measurement and detection.</p></p>
