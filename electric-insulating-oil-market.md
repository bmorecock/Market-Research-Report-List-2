<p><p>Electric insulating oil is available in various types, including mineral oil-based, silicone-based, and bio-based oils. Mineral oil-based insulating oils are the most commonly used due to their high dielectric strength and cost-effectiveness. Silicone-based oils offer better thermal stability and oxidation resistance. Bio-based oils, on the other hand, are environmentally friendly and biodegradable. Each type of insulating oil caters to different needs and preferences, providing options for industries looking to optimize their electrical insulation systems.</p></p>
