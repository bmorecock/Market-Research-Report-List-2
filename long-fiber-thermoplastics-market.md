<p><p>Long Fiber Thermoplastics (LFT) are available in various types, with popular ones being PP (Polypropylene), PA (Polyamide), PEEK (Polyether Ether Ketone), PPA (Polyphthalamide), and other variants. Each type offers different properties and performance characteristics suitable for various applications. PP is known for its high impact resistance and flexibility, while PA provides high strength and durability. PEEK offers excellent thermal stability and chemical resistance, making it ideal for demanding applications. PPA combines the best features of other types, making it a versatile choice.</p></p>
