<p><ul><li>Vertical Surface Planer</li><li>Horizontal Surface Planer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vertical surface planers are machines used for surfacing large panels and boards in a vertical orientation, making them suitable for processing longer and wider pieces of wood. On the other hand, horizontal surface planers are designed for smaller workpieces and are more commonly used for shaping edges, rebating, and chamfering. Both types of planers are essential tools in woodworking and carpentry, offering versatility and efficiency in achieving smooth and flat surfaces on various types of wood.</p></p>
