<p><p>Lighting ballasts market includes various types of lamp electrodes such as Preheat Operation Lamp Electrodes, Rapid Start Operation Lamp Electrodes, and Instant Start Operation Lamp Electrodes. Preheat operation lamp electrodes require a starter to preheat the lamp before ignition, while rapid start operation lamp electrodes provide continuous power to maintain the lamp's brightness. Instant start operation lamp electrodes do not require preheating and provide instant illumination. These different types cater to different lighting needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
