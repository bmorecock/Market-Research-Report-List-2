<p><p>Motor Control Centers (MCCs) are essential components in industrial automation and process control systems. There are two main types of MCCs based on voltage requirements: Low Voltage Motor Control Centers (LV MCC) and Medium Voltage Motor Control Centers (MV MCC). LV MCCs are typically used in commercial and industrial applications where the voltage requirement is below 1000 volts, while MV MCCs are used in applications requiring voltage levels above 1000 volts. Both types of MCCs provide power distribution, motor protection, and control functions for various equipment and machinery in industrial settings.</p></p>
