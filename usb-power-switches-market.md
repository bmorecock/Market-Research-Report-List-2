<p><p>USB Power Switches are commonly categorized into two main market types: Single Channel and Dual Channel USB Power Switches. Single Channel switches are designed to control power to a single USB port or device, providing simple on/off functionality. On the other hand, Dual Channel switches are able to control power to two separate USB ports or devices simultaneously, offering greater flexibility and convenience. Both types of switches are essential components for managing power distribution in various electronic devices and applications.</p></p>
