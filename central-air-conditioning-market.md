<p><strong>The Central Air Conditioning Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Expansion (DX) Central Air Conditioning Systems</li><li>Central Plant/Chilled Water Central Air Conditioning System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Direct Expansion (DX) Central Air Conditioning Systems are a common type of central air conditioning system where the refrigerant directly cools the air in the indoor unit before being circulated throughout the building. On the other hand, Central Plant/Chilled Water Central Air Conditioning Systems use a central plant to chill water, which is then pumped to cooling coils in individual spaces to cool the air. Both systems have their own unique advantages and are selected based on factors such as building size, cooling requirements, and energy efficiency goals.</p></p>
