In the market, there are two main types of Transparent OLED displays: AMOLED (active-matrix organic light-emitting diode) and PMOLED (passive-matrix organic light-emitting diode). AMOLED displays offer better color accuracy and contrast, making them suitable for high-end applications, while PMOLED displays are more cost-effective and simpler in design, making them suitable for simpler display requirements.</p></p>
