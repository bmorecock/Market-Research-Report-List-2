<p><strong>The Uninterruptible Power Supplies Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Off-line or standby</li><li>Line-interactive</li><li>Online or double-conversion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Uninterruptible Power Supplies (UPS) are available in three main types: Off-line or standby, Line-interactive, and Online or double-conversion. Off-line UPS systems are the most basic, providing protection against power outages by switching to battery power when necessary. Line-interactive UPS systems offer additional voltage regulation and can handle small fluctuations in power. Online UPS systems provide the highest level of protection, continuously converting AC power to DC and back again, ensuring a clean and stable power supply at all times.</p></p>
