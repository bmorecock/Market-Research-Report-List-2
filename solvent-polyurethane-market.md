Polyether type polyurethanes are known for their excellent resistance to water and humidity, making them suitable for outdoor applications. Polyester type polyurethanes offer superior resistance to chemicals and abrasion, making them ideal for industrial environments. Mixed type polyurethanes combine characteristics of both polyether and polyester types, offering a balance of properties suitable for a wide range of applications.</p></p>
