<p><strong>The Spray Foam Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Light-density</li><li>Medium-density</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spray foam market types can be categorized into light-density and medium-density. Light-density spray foam is typically used for interior insulation, as it expands and seals small gaps effectively. It is also cost-effective and easy to work with. On the other hand, medium-density spray foam is used for exterior applications, such as roofing and insulation in areas that require more structural support. It offers higher R-values and better thermal performance compared to light-density foam. Both types cater to different market needs and applications.</p></p>
