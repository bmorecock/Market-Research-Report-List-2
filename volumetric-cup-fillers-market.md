<p><p>Volumetric cup fillers are used in packaging and filling operations to accurately measure and dispense products. There are three main types of volumetric cup fillers: Manual, Semi-automatic, and Automatic. Manual fillers require an operator to manually place and remove containers under the filling spout. Semi-automatic fillers involve some automation in the filling process, while still requiring operator intervention. Automatic fillers are fully automated and can operate without direct human involvement, increasing efficiency and productivity in packaging operations.</p></p>
