<p><p>Cellular V2X communication encompasses three main market types. Vehicle-to-vehicle (V2V) communication involves vehicles sharing information to improve safety and traffic efficiency. Vehicle-to-Infrastructure (V2I) communication involves vehicles interacting with infrastructure such as traffic lights and signs to enhance traffic flow. Vehicle-to-Pedestrian (V2P) communication allows vehicles to communicate with pedestrians or other vulnerable road users to prevent accidents and ensure safe passage. These market types all aim to enable safer and more efficient transportation systems through advanced connectivity technologies.</p></p>
