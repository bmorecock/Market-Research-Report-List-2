In Sleeve Gastrectomy, a portion of the stomach is removed to restrict food intake. Gastric Bypass involves creating a small pouch from the stomach and rerouting the small intestine to limit calorie absorption. Gastric Banding involves placing a band around the stomach to reduce the amount of food it can hold. Other surgeries include procedures like gastric balloon insertion or biliopancreatic diversion.</p></p>
