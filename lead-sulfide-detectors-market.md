<p><p>Lead Sulfide Detectors are used in various market types based on the wavelength range they detect. Near-infrared (NIR) & Shortwave Infrared (SWIR) detectors operate in the 0.4-3um wavelength range, ideal for industrial and scientific applications. Mid-wave Infrared (MWIR) detectors cover the 3-5um range, commonly used in defense and surveillance systems. Long-wave Infrared (LWIR) detectors detect wavelengths from 8-14um, making them suitable for thermal imaging and night vision applications. Each market type offers unique capabilities and applications based on the wavelength range they operate in.</p></p>
