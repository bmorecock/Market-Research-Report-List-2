<p><p>Hermetic headers are specialized connectors used in hermetically sealed electronic packages to ensure a gas-tight seal. There are two main types of hermetic headers in the market: multi-pin hermetic headers and single-pin hermetic headers. Multi-pin hermetic headers feature multiple pins for connecting various electronic components, while single-pin hermetic headers have a single pin for simpler applications. Both types play crucial roles in maintaining the integrity of the sealed electronic package and preventing contamination from external factors.</p></p>
