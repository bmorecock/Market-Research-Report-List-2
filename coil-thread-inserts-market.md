<p><p>Coil thread inserts are available in two main types - Tanged Thread Inserts and Tangless Thread Inserts. Tanged thread inserts require a tang for installation and removal, providing additional support and facilitate easier installation. On the other hand, Tangless thread inserts do not have a tang, making them easier to install and remove without the risk of tang breakage. Both types offer secure threaded connections and are commonly used in industries where strong, reliable threads are essential.</p></p>
