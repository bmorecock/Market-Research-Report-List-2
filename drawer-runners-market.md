<p><p>Drawer runners are commonly categorized into two main types: undermount drawer runners and ball-bearing drawer runners. Undermount drawer runners are mounted underneath the drawer, providing a sleek and hidden look. They offer smooth and silent operation, making them popular for modern and high-end cabinetry. On the other hand, ball-bearing drawer runners utilize ball bearings to support the weight of the drawer, offering durability and ease of movement. Both types cater to different preferences and requirements in the furniture industry.</p></p>
