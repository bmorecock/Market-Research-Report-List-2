<p><p>Converter modules market includes three main types: DC-DC converter module, DC-AC converter module, and AC-DC converter module. DC-DC converter modules help convert one DC voltage to another DC voltage, suitable for applications like battery-powered devices. DC-AC converter modules transform DC voltage to AC voltage, commonly used in power supplies for electronic devices. AC-DC converter modules do the opposite, converting AC voltage to DC voltage, often used in household electronic appliances. Each type of converter module serves different purposes based on voltage conversion requirements.</p></p>
