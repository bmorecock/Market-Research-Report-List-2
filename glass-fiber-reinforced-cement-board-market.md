<p><p>Glass Fiber Reinforced Cement Board market offers two main types; Lightweight Flat and Lightweight Hollow Slats. Lightweight Flat boards are flat panels with high strength and durability, ideal for applications where weight is a concern but strength cannot be compromised. Lightweight Hollow Slats, on the other hand, offer a combination of strength and flexibility due to their hollow design. They are commonly used in applications where a combination of load-bearing capacity and flexibility is required. Both types cater to different needs in the construction industry.</p></p>
