<p><p>Aircraft brake discs market can be divided into two main types: Carbon-Ceramic and Carbon-Carbon. Carbon-Ceramic discs are made from a composite material of carbon fibers and ceramic, offering high thermal resistance and durability. These discs are commonly used in high-performance aircraft. Carbon-Carbon discs, on the other hand, are made entirely of carbon material, providing even higher thermal resistance and reduced wear. They are preferred for extreme operating conditions and are often used in military and aerospace applications.</p></p>
