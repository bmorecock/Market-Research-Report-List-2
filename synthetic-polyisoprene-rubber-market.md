<p><p>Synthetic Polyisoprene Rubber comes in two different types - Cis and Trans. Cis-Polyisoprene has a linear molecular structure that allows for better elasticity and resilience, making it ideal for applications requiring high stretchability and durability. On the other hand, Trans-Polyisoprene has a more rigid molecular structure, providing better heat resistance and stability. Each type has its own unique properties that make them suitable for different industrial and commercial applications, catering to diverse needs in the market.</p></p>
