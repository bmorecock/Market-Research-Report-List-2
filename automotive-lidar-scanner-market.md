<p><ul><li>Mechanical Lidar</li><li>Solid State Lidar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive LiDAR Scanner market consists of two main types: Mechanical LiDAR and Solid State LiDAR. Mechanical LiDAR systems use moving parts to scan the surrounding environment, which can provide high-resolution images but are more prone to wear and tear. On the other hand, Solid State LiDAR systems do not have any moving parts, making them more durable and compact. They are also more cost-effective to manufacture. Both types of LiDAR technology are crucial for autonomous driving systems in the automotive industry.</p></p>
