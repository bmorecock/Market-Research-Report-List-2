<p><p>Photovoltaic Off-grid Power Generation System refers to systems that generate electricity from solar energy and store it in batteries for use in remote areas without access to the grid. On-off Grid Energy Storage System combines traditional grid-connected systems with battery storage to provide backup power during outages. Photovoltaic Grid-connected Energy Storage System involves storing excess solar energy in batteries and feeding it back into the grid when needed. Microgrid Energy Storage System is used in small-scale localized grids to balance supply and demand.</p></p>
