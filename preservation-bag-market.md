<p><p>Preservation bags are available in different types such as PE (Polyethylene), PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride), and PVDC (Polyvinylidene Chloride). PE preservation bags are ideal for storing everyday items due to their durability and flexibility. PVC preservation bags have good resistance to moisture and chemicals, making them suitable for food packaging. PVDC preservation bags are known for their high barrier properties, making them ideal for long-term storage of perishable goods. Each type of preservation bag caters to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
