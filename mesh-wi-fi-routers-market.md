<p><p>Mesh Wi-Fi routers come in various market types based on the number of frequency bands they operate on. Bi-Band routers operate on two frequency bands, typically 2.4GHz and 5GHz, providing basic coverage for most homes. Tri-Band routers operate on three frequency bands, offering better performance and capacity in larger homes or offices. Quad-Band routers operate on four frequency bands, providing maximum performance for heavy network traffic and demanding users. Each market type caters to different needs and usage scenarios.</p></p>
