<p><p>Low Noise Synthesizers are used in various applications, with three primary market types: Frequency Modulation, Phase Modulation, and Pulse Modulation. Frequency Modulation involves varying the frequency of the output signal to generate different tones. Phase Modulation changes the phase of the signal to produce variations in sound. Pulse Modulation involves turning the signal on and off rapidly to create different pulses of sound. Each market type caters to different needs and preferences in the low noise synthesizer industry.</p></p>
