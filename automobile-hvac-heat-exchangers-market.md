<p><p>Automobile HVAC Heat Exchangers can be categorized into three main types: condensers, heat cores, and evaporators. Condensers are responsible for removing heat from refrigerant vapor, converting it into liquid form. Heat cores transfer heat from the engine coolant to the HVAC system. Evaporators absorb heat from the air inside the vehicle, cooling it down. Each type plays a crucial role in regulating the temperature inside the vehicle and ensuring the comfort of the passengers.</p></p>
