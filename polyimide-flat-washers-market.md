<p><p>Polyimide flat washers can be classified into two main types: ordinary washers and reinforced washers. </p><p>Ordinary washers are typically made from pure polyimide material and are used for standard applications where only moderate pressure and heat resistance are required. </p><p>Reinforced washers, on the other hand, are manufactured with additional reinforcement materials such as glass fibers or graphite to enhance their mechanical strength and thermal properties. This makes them suitable for high-pressure and high-temperature applications where ordinary washers may not suffice.</p></p>
