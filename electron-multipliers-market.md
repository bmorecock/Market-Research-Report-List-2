<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Electron Multipliers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Channel Electron Multipliers (CEM)</li><li>Continuous-dynode multipliers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electron Multipliers are used in analytical instruments to amplify electron signals, with two popular types being Channel Electron Multipliers (CEM) and Continuous-dynode multipliers. CEMs utilize a series of channels to amplify electrons, providing high sensitivity and fast response times. Continuous-dynode multipliers use a continuous chain of dynodes to multiply electrons, offering high gain and low noise performance. Both types are widely used in mass spectrometry, electron microscopy, and other analytical applications for their excellent performance in detecting low levels of electrons.</p></p>
