<p><strong>The Loom Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Jet Loom</li><li>Water Jet Loom</li><li>Rapier and Projectile Loom</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Air jet looms are known for their high weaving speed and efficiency, making them ideal for mass production of simple fabrics. Water jet looms use water as a medium to propel the weft yarn across the warp, suitable for weaving lightweight and delicate fabrics. Rapier looms utilize flexible rapiers to insert the weft yarn, making them versatile and suitable for a wide range of fabrics. Projectile looms use a shuttle-like device to insert the weft yarn, making them capable of weaving complex patterns and heavy fabrics.</p></p>
