This is due to regulations such as the European Union's MAC Directive, which mandates the use of low GWP refrigerants in vehicles. As a result, automotive manufacturers are increasingly choosing R-1234yf refrigerant over traditional high GWP refrigerants.</p><p>Overall, the R-1234yf refrigerant market is poised for steady growth in the coming years, driven by increasing awareness of environmental issues and the need for sustainable refrigerant solutions.</p></p>
