<p><p>LED Visible Light Communication System market is segmented into two primary types: PC-LED and RGB-LED. PC-LED, or phosphor-converted LED, utilizes a blue LED chip coated with a yellow phosphor to create white light, offering high efficiency and cost-effective solutions for indoor lighting. On the other hand, RGB-LED, or red-green-blue LED, produces a wide range of colors by combining different intensities of red, green, and blue light sources, making it ideal for applications requiring color customization and dynamic lighting effects.</p></p>
