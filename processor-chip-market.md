<p><p>Processor chips can be categorized into three main types: digital chips, analog chips, and hybrid chips. Digital chips process information in the form of binary data, performing tasks like arithmetic operations and logical functions. Analog chips, on the other hand, deal with continuous signals such as sound and light, enabling functions like amplification and modulation. Hybrid chips combine elements of both digital and analog chips, offering versatility in processing various types of signals. Each market type caters to specific applications and industries based on their unique functionalities.</p></p>
