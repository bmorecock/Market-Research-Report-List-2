<p><p>Honing machines are used for precision finishing of metal parts such as cylinders, pipes, and gears. There are two main types of honing machines: Horizontal Honing Machines and Vertical Honing Machines. Horizontal honing machines are suitable for longer workpieces and provide higher productivity, while vertical honing machines are ideal for smaller workpieces and offer better accuracy. Both types of machines use abrasive stones to remove small amounts of material, creating a smooth and precise surface finish.</p></p>
