<p><p>Automotive Hardware-in-the-Loop Testing is a method used in the automotive industry to simulate and validate the performance of electronic control systems in a virtual environment. By testing electronic control systems in a controlled and repeatable environment, Automotive Hardware-in-the-Loop Testing helps identify and resolve potential issues before they occur in actual vehicles, thereby reducing development time and costs.</p><p>The Automotive Hardware-in-the-Loop Testing market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.7% during the forecast period.
