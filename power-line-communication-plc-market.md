<p><p>Power Line Communication (PLC) enables data transmission over existing power lines, offering two main market types: Narrowband PLC and Broadband PLC. Narrowband PLC operates at lower frequencies, ideal for smart metering and home automation applications. Broadband PLC operates at higher frequencies, providing faster data speeds suitable for applications like internet access and video streaming. Both market types utilize PLC technology to leverage existing power infrastructure for communication and data transmission purposes.</p></p>
