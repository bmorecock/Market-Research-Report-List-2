<p><p>Biobased paints and coatings are classified into various types based on their composition and application. Acrylic biobased paints and coatings are known for their fast drying time and high gloss finish. Bio-PLA biobased paints and coatings are made from renewable resources like corn starch and are biodegradable. PHA biobased paints and coatings are eco-friendly options derived from microbes and offer good adhesion and flexibility.</p></p>
