<p><ul><li>Organic Coating</li><li>Inorganic Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical fiber coating market consists of two main types: organic coating and inorganic coating. Organic coatings are made from polymers such as acrylate or polyimide, providing flexibility and ease of handling during installation. Inorganic coatings, on the other hand, are typically made from materials like metal or ceramics, offering higher resistance to temperature and humidity. Both types of coatings play a crucial role in protecting the delicate optical fibers and ensuring optimal performance in various applications.</p></p>
