<p><p>Double lumen bronchial tubes are medical devices used for lung isolation during surgeries. The left double lumen bronchial tubes are specifically designed for isolating the left lung, while the right double lumen bronchial tubes are used for isolating the right lung. These tubes allow for independent ventilation of each lung, facilitating better surgical access and reducing the risk of complications during procedures. Both types of tubes are essential tools for anesthesiologists and surgeons performing thoracic surgeries requiring lung isolation.</p></p>
