<p><strong>The Aluminium Foams Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ex-situ Bonded Aluminium Foams</li><li>In-situ Bonded Aluminium Foams</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aluminium foams on the market can be categorized into two main types: ex-situ bonded aluminium foams and in-situ bonded aluminium foams. Ex-situ bonded aluminium foams are manufactured by bonding a pre-made aluminium foam to another material, while in-situ bonded aluminium foams involve the foam being formed and bonded simultaneously within its final structure. Both types offer unique properties and applications, with ex-situ bonded foams being better suited for specific applications requiring increased strength, while in-situ bonded foams are more versatile and customizable for various uses.</p></p>
