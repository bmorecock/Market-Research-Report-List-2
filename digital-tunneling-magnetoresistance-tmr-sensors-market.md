<p><p>Digital Tunneling Magnetoresistance (TMR) Sensors are classified into three main types based on their sensing capabilities - Omnipolar, Bipolar, and Unipolar. Omnipolar sensors can detect both north and south magnetic fields, making them versatile for various applications. Bipolar sensors can only detect one polarity, while unipolar sensors can only detect either north or south magnetic fields. Each type has its own advantages and limitations, catering to different needs in industries such as automotive, consumer electronics, and industrial automation.</p></p>
