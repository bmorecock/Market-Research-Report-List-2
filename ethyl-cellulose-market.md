<p><strong>The Ethyl Cellulose Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ethyl cellulose is classified into two types in the market: Type I and Type II. Type I ethyl cellulose is a high-viscosity grade with excellent film-forming properties, making it ideal for use in coatings, binders, and adhesives. On the other hand, Type II ethyl cellulose is a low-viscosity grade commonly used in pharmaceutical applications such as controlled-release drug delivery systems. Both types of ethyl cellulose have different viscosities and are suitable for specific purposes based on their properties and applications in various industries.</p></p>
