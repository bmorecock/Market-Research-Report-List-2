<p><p>Wood skirting boards are commonly made from three main types of fiberboard: low density fiberboard (LDF), medium-density fiberboard (MDF), and high-density fiberboard (HDF). LDF skirting boards are cost-effective but less durable, making them suitable for low-traffic areas. MDF skirting boards offer a balance between affordability and durability, making them a popular choice for most households. HDF skirting boards are the most expensive but also the most durable, making them ideal for high-traffic areas or commercial buildings.</p></p>
