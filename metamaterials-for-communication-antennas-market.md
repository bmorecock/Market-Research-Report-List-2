<p><p>Metamaterials for Communication Antennas Market Types include Negative Index Metamaterials (NIM), Single Negative (SNG) Metamaterials, and Hyperbolic Metamaterials (HMM). NIM has a negative refractive index, allowing for unique properties like superlensing. SNG Metamaterials have negative permittivity or permeability, enabling control over electromagnetic waves. HMM exhibit hyperbolic dispersion, ideal for controlling light at nanoscale levels. These types of metamaterials offer innovative solutions for enhancing communication antennas by manipulating electromagnetic waves in novel ways.</p></p>
