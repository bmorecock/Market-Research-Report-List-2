<p><p>XLPE Cable & Wire Market is segmented into four types based on voltage levels: LV Cables (Low Voltage), MV Cables (Medium Voltage), HV Cables (High Voltage), and EHV Cables (Extra High Voltage). LV Cables are used for voltages up to 1kV, while MV Cables are used for voltages ranging from 1kV to 33kV. HV Cables are used for voltages between 33kV and 132kV, whereas EHV Cables are used for voltages exceeding 132kV. Each type serves a specific purpose in transmitting electricity over different distances and applications.</p></p>
