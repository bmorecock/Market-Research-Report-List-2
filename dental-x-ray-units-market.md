<p><p>Dental X-Ray Units market consists of two main types - Intraoral X-ray Unit and Extraoral X-ray Unit. Intraoral X-ray Units are used to take X-rays inside the mouth and are commonly used for detecting cavities, bone loss, and infections. On the other hand, Extraoral X-ray Units are used to capture images of the entire mouth, including the jaw and skull. These units are often used for more complex dental procedures such as orthodontic treatment planning and detecting tumors.</p></p>
