<p><p>The Exhaust Expansion Joints market includes two main types: Flange Type and Welding Type. Flange Type expansion joints are connected using flanges that allow for easy installation and disassembly. They are commonly used in pipelines where flexibility is required. Welding Type expansion joints are welded directly onto the pipeline, providing a permanent and strong connection. These expansion joints are suitable for high-pressure applications where durability is crucial. Both types play a significant role in maintaining the integrity and efficiency of exhaust systems.</p></p>
