<p><ul><li>Single Mode Optical Fiber</li><li>Multimode Optical Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical fiber market is divided into two main types: Single Mode Optical Fiber and Multimode Optical Fiber. Single Mode Optical Fiber is designed for long-distance data transmission with a single light path, making it ideal for applications requiring high bandwidth. Multimode Optical Fiber, on the other hand, is used for shorter distances and allows multiple light paths to pass through simultaneously, making it suitable for local area networks and data centers. Both types play a critical role in the telecommunications industry, providing reliable and efficient data transmission.</p></p>
