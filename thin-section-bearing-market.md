<p><p>Thin section bearings are commonly divided into four types in the market: Radial Ball Bearings, Angular Contact Bearings, Four-point Contact Bearings, and Other market. Radial Ball Bearings are designed for radial loads, while Angular Contact Bearings can handle both radial and axial loads. Four-point Contact Bearings offer high stiffness and low torque, making them suitable for applications requiring precise positioning. The Other market category includes specialty thin section bearings with unique features for specific industries or applications.</p></p>
