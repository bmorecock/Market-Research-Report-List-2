<p><strong>The Stormwater Treatment System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Screening System</li><li>Separation System</li><li>Filtration System</li><li>Infiltration System</li><li>Retention System</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stormwater treatment systems help to remove pollutants and debris from stormwater before it enters natural water bodies. Screening systems use physical barriers to remove large particles, while separation systems separate solids and liquids based on density. Filtration systems use materials like sand or vegetation to filter out pollutants. Infiltration systems allow stormwater to seep into the ground, while retention systems store stormwater for gradual release. Other types of stormwater treatment systems include biofiltration, constructed wetlands, and oil-water separators. Each system plays a crucial role in protecting water quality.</p></p>
