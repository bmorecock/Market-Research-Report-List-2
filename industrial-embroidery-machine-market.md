<p><ul><li>Single-Head Embroidery Machine</li><li>Multi-Head Embroidery Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial embroidery machines come in two main types: single-head and multi-head machines. Single-head machines are ideal for small businesses or individual crafters who have fewer orders to fulfill. These machines are more affordable and take up less space. On the other hand, multi-head machines are designed for larger production volumes and are able to embroider multiple items simultaneously, making them ideal for medium to large-sized businesses. Both types of machines offer precision stitching and a wide range of customization options.</p></p>
