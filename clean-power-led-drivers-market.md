<p><p>Clean power LED drivers are used to power LED lights efficiently and effectively. The two main types of drivers in the market are constant current and regulated current drivers. Constant current drivers ensure a consistent flow of electricity to the LED, preventing fluctuations in light output. Regulated current drivers, on the other hand, maintain a set current level, regardless of voltage changes. Both types of drivers help to provide stable and reliable power to LED lights, optimizing their performance and lifespan.</p></p>
