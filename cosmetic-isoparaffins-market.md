<p><p>Cosmetic isoparaffins are primarily categorized into four types: Isododecane, Isohexadecane, Isoeicosane, and others. Isododecane is a lightweight and fast-evaporating isoparaffin commonly used in cosmetic products to reduce greasiness and enhance spreadability. Isohexadecane is a high-purity isoparaffin that provides a silky and smooth skin feel. Isoeicosane is a long-chain isoparaffin that offers excellent moisturizing properties. The "Others" category includes various isoparaffins with unique characteristics and uses in cosmetics.</p></p>
