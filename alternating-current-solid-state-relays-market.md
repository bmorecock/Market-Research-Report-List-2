<p><p>Alternating Current Solid State Relays are categorized into three main types based on their mounting options. PCB Mount relays are directly mounted onto a printed circuit board for compact and space-saving applications. Panel Mount relays are installed onto the front or back panel of a control cabinet for easy access and maintenance. Din Rail Mount relays are mounted onto a standard DIN rail for efficient organization and installation in industrial settings. Each type offers unique benefits in terms of space utilization, accessibility, and installation ease.</p></p>
