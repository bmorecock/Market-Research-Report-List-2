<p><p>In-line automatic textile screen print machines are designed for continuous production, with garments moving through the machine in a straight line for efficient printing. Carousel automatic textile screen print machines have multiple print stations arranged in a circular fashion, allowing for simultaneous printing of multiple colors or designs. The market for in-line machines caters to high-volume production needs, while the carousel market caters to smaller-scale productions requiring flexibility and customization. Both types of machines offer advanced technology and automation for fast and precise textile printing.</p></p>
