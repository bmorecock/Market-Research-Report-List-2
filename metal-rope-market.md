<p><p>Metal rope market is divided into two main types: insulated and non-insulated. Insulated metal rope is designed to provide protection against electrical currents and prevent electric shocks, making it ideal for use in construction, power transmission, and telecommunications industries. On the other hand, non-insulated metal rope is commonly used for lifting and rigging applications, as it offers strength and durability without the added insulation layer. Both types cater to different industrial needs and requirements in the market.</p></p>
