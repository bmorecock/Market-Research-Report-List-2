<p><p>Hand pumps are available in various types to cater to different needs. Rotary pumps are efficient for transferring fluids easily, while lever action pumps are suitable for dispensing liquids quickly. Piston pumps are commonly used for pumping water from wells. Drum siphon pumps are ideal for transferring liquids from containers. Hydraulic hand pumps are used for heavy-duty applications where high pressure is required. Each type of hand pump serves a specific purpose and is designed to provide convenient and effective pumping solutions.</p></p>
