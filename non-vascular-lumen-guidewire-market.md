<p><p>Non-vascular lumen guidewires are medical devices used to guide the placement of catheters in non-vascular structures within the body. Straight guidewires are rigid and straight, providing stability and precision in navigating tight spaces. Elbow guidewires have a pre-formed bend that allows for easier navigation around curves and corners. Both types of guidewires play a crucial role in various medical procedures, such as endoscopy and bronchoscopy, by providing a pathway for catheter insertion. They are essential tools in minimally invasive surgeries and diagnostic interventions.</p></p>
