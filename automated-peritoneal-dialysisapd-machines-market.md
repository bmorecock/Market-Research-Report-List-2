<p><p>Automated Peritoneal Dialysis (APD) machines are medical devices used for kidney dialysis treatment. There are two main types of APD machines in the market: Power Type and Gravity Type. Power Type machines use an electrical pump to control the flow of dialysis solution into and out of the peritoneal cavity, providing more precise control over the dialysis process. In contrast, Gravity Type machines rely on the force of gravity to move the dialysis solution, offering a simpler and more cost-effective option for patients.</p></p>
