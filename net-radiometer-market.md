<p><strong>The Net Radiometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>2-Component Net Radiometer</li><li>4-Component Net Radiometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Net radiometers are instruments used to measure the difference between incoming and outgoing radiation levels in the atmosphere. 2-component net radiometers measure the net radiation by comparing the levels of shortwave and longwave radiation. On the other hand, 4-component net radiometers also take into account the directionality of the radiation, making them more accurate in determining the energy balance in the atmosphere. Both types of net radiometers are essential tools in studying the Earth's energy budget and climate change.</p></p>
