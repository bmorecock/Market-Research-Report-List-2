<p><p>Ketones market is divided into two main types: Saturated Ketones and Unsaturated Ketones. Saturated Ketones have double bonds connected to two carbon atoms, making them relatively stable and less reactive. They are widely used in the production of solvents, fragrances, and pharmaceuticals. On the other hand, Unsaturated Ketones contain double bonds connecting three or more carbon atoms, making them more reactive. They are commonly used in the manufacture of resins, plastics, and rubber products. Both types play crucial roles in various industries and applications.</p></p>
