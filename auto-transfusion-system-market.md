<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Auto Transfusion System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Autotransfusion Systems</li><li>Discontinuous Autotransfusion Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Continuous Autotransfusion Systems are used to continuously collect, filter, and transfuse a patient's own blood during surgery. This type of system provides a continuous supply of blood without the need for external blood sources. On the other hand, Discontinuous Autotransfusion Systems are used to collect and store blood from a patient during surgery, which can then be reinfused back into the patient later on. This type of system allows for the collection and transfusion of larger volumes of blood at one time.</p></p>
