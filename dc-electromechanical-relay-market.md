<p><ul><li>PCB Mounting</li><li>DIN Rail Mounting</li><li>Panel-mount</li><li>Surface-mount</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>DC Electromechanical Relay market includes various types based on mounting options. PCB mounting relays are directly mounted on printed circuit boards, while DIN rail mounting relays are secured to DIN rails in industrial applications. Panel-mount relays are installed on control panels for easy access. Surface-mount relays are mounted on the surface of circuit boards. Other markets include plug-in relays, socket mount relays, and flange mount relays, each catering to specific installation requirements and industry applications.</p></p>
