<p><strong>The Laboratory Hydrogen Generators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Membrane Technology</li><li>PSA Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laboratory hydrogen generators are available in two main types - Membrane Technology and PSA (Pressure Swing Adsorption) Technology. Membrane Technology operates by separating hydrogen molecules from other gases using a selective membrane, while PSA Technology works by adsorbing gases onto a solid surface under high pressure and releasing them under lower pressure. Both types are commonly used in laboratory settings to produce pure hydrogen gas for various applications such as chromatography and fuel cell testing.</p></p>
