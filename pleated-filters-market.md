<p><strong>The Pleated Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MERV 7 and Below</li><li>MERV 8</li><li>MERV 9</li><li>MERV 10</li><li>MERV 11</li><li>MERV 12</li><li>MERV 13</li><li>MERV 14 and Above</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pleated filters are categorized by their Minimum Efficiency Reporting Value (MERV) rating, which indicates their effectiveness in filtering particles. Filters with a MERV rating of 7 and below are basic filters suitable for light dust and debris. MERV 8 filters offer improved filtration for small particles like mold spores. As the MERV rating increases, so does the filter's ability to capture finer particles, with MERV 14 and above filters providing high efficiency for capturing tiny particles like bacteria and viruses.</p></p>
