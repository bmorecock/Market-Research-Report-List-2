<p><p>Miniature ball bearings market is divided into two types based on their design and construction: Open Miniature Ball Bearings and Dust Over Miniature Ball Bearings. Open Miniature Ball Bearings have no shields or seals, allowing for easy cleaning and maintenance. On the other hand, Dust Over Miniature Ball Bearings are equipped with shields or seals to protect the internal components from dirt, dust, and other contaminants, making them ideal for applications in which cleanliness is a priority.</p></p>
