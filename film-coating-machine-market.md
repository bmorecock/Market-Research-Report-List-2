<p><p>Film coating machine market has two main types: Evaporation Coating Equipment and Sputter Coating Equipment. Evaporation Coating Equipment involves heating a material until it evaporates and then condenses onto the substrate. This method is commonly used for producing thin films with high purity. On the other hand, Sputter Coating Equipment involves bombarding a target material with high-energy ions to eject atoms onto the substrate, creating a thin film. Both types of equipment are essential for different applications in industries such as electronics, automotive, and aerospace.</p></p>
