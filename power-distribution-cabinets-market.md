<p><ul><li>Low-voltage</li><li>Middle-voltage</li><li>High-voltage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Power distribution cabinets are categorized based on the voltage levels they operate at. Low-voltage cabinets are designed for distributing electricity at voltages below 1kV, typically found in residential and commercial buildings. Middle-voltage cabinets operate at voltages ranging from 1kV to 33kV, commonly used in industrial applications and large buildings. High-voltage cabinets are used for voltages above 33kV, typically seen in power plants and utility grids. Each type of cabinet is designed to handle specific voltage levels and applications efficiently and safely.</p></p>
