<p><p>Beverage carton packaging machines come in various types to cater to different packaging needs. Horizontal end side-load machines load cartons from the top or side, while top-load machines place cartons from the top. Wraparound machines wrap cartons around the product, ensuring a tight seal. Vertical leaflet machines insert leaflets into cartons vertically, while vertical sleeve machines insert sleeves around cartons. Each type of machine offers unique functionalities to meet specific packaging requirements in the beverage industry.</p></p>
