Throttle Body Fuel Injector sprays fuel into the throttle body, Direct Fuel Injector directly injects fuel into the combustion chamber, Sequential Fuel Injector injects fuel in a specific sequence, and Port Fuel Injector injects fuel into the intake port of each cylinder.</p></p>
