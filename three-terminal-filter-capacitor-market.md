<p><p>In the three terminal filter capacitor market, two common types are the π-type filter and the T-type filter. The π-type filter consists of a series capacitor and two shunt capacitors connected in a T configuration. This configuration helps in reducing high-frequency noise and improving filtering efficiency. On the other hand, the T-type filter consists of a series capacitor and two shunt capacitors connected in a π configuration. This setup also helps in mitigating noise and enhancing filtering capabilities in electronic circuits.</p></p>
