<p><p>Ultraviolet curing coating market consists of various types such as monomers, oligomers, photoinitiators, and PU dispersions. Monomers are the building blocks of UV coatings, while oligomers provide the desired properties and functionality. Photoinitiators help in the curing process by initiating the reaction upon exposure to UV light. PU dispersions are water-based coatings that offer excellent adhesion and durability. Each of these types plays a crucial role in the formulation and application of UV curing coatings in different industries.</p></p>
