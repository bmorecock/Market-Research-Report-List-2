<p><strong>The Liquid Applied Membrane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bituminous Membrane</li><li>Elastomeric Membrane</li><li>Cementitious Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Liquid Applied Membrane market is divided into three main types - Bituminous Membrane, Elastomeric Membrane, and Cementitious Membrane. Bituminous Membrane is made from bitumen and offers excellent waterproofing properties. Elastomeric Membrane is highly flexible and durable, making it ideal for areas with high movement. Cementitious Membrane is a cement-based coating that provides waterproofing and protection against chemical attacks. Each type has its own unique properties and benefits, catering to different needs in the construction industry.</p></p>
