<p><p>Industrial safety footwear market is divided into two main types: shoes and boots. Safety shoes are low-cut footwear that provide protection to the foot and ankle, commonly worn in industries where there is a low risk of heavy objects falling. Safety boots, on the other hand, provide additional protection to the lower leg and are commonly worn in industries with a higher risk of injuries such as construction and manufacturing. Both types of footwear are designed to meet specific safety standards and provide workers with the necessary protection in hazardous work environments.</p></p>
