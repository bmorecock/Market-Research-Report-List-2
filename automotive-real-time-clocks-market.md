<p><ul><li>I2C-bus RTCs</li><li>SPI RTCs</li><li>Integrated Quartz Crystal RTCs</li><li>Ultra-Low-Power RTCs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Real-time Clocks (RTC) market offers various types including I2C-bus RTCs, SPI RTCs, Integrated Quartz Crystal RTCs, and Ultra-Low-Power RTCs. I2C-bus RTCs provide a simple two-wire interface for communication. SPI RTCs use a Serial Peripheral Interface for high-speed data transfer. Integrated Quartz Crystal RTCs have a built-in crystal oscillator for timekeeping accuracy. Ultra-Low-Power RTCs are designed to minimize power consumption, making them ideal for automotive applications where battery life is crucial. Each type offers unique features to cater to different requirements in the automotive industry.</p></p>
