<p><p>Laser films are primarily categorized into three types based on the material used: PET laser film, BOPP laser film, and PVC laser film. PET laser film is made from polyethylene terephthalate, BOPP laser film is made from biaxially oriented polypropylene, and PVC laser film is made from polyvinyl chloride. Each type of laser film offers unique properties and characteristics, making them suitable for various applications such as labeling, packaging, printing, and lamination. These films provide high clarity, excellent printability, and enhanced protection for products.</p></p>
