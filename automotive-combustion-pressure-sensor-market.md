<p><strong>The Automotive Combustion Pressure Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic Type</li><li>Piezoresistive Effect Type</li><li>Resistance - Strain Effect Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive combustion pressure sensors are crucial components in monitoring the pressure inside the engine cylinders. Three main types are commonly used - Electromagnetic type, Piezoresistive effect type, and Resistance-strain effect type. The Electromagnetic type uses electromagnetic induction to measure pressure changes, Piezoresistive effect type utilizes the change in electrical resistance due to pressure, and Resistance-strain effect type measures pressure by detecting the strain on a sensitive element. Each type has its advantages and applications in various automotive systems for efficient and accurate pressure monitoring.</p></p>
