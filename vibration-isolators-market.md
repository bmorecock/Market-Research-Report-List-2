<p><p>Vibration isolators play a crucial role in reducing vibration and noise in various mechanical systems. The market for vibration isolators can be classified into different types, including elastomeric isolators, mechanical isolators, air isolator mounts, compact pneumatic isolators, and others. Elastomeric isolators use rubber or elastomer materials to absorb vibrations, while mechanical isolators use springs or mechanical devices. Air isolator mounts use compressed air to isolate vibrations, and compact pneumatic isolators are smaller versions of air isolators. Other types of isolators include hydraulic and wire rope isolators.</p></p>
