<p><p>Graphene Oxide Films market consists of two main types - Single Layer Film and Double Layer Film. Single Layer Film is a thin film composed of a single layer of graphene oxide, offering high transparency and flexibility. Double Layer Film, on the other hand, consists of two layers of graphene oxide, providing enhanced mechanical strength and barrier properties. Both types of films are widely used in various applications such as electronics, optics, and energy storage due to their unique properties and versatility.</p></p>
