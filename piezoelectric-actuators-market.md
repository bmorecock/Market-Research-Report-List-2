<p><ul><li>Stack Actuators</li><li>Stripe Actuators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Piezoelectric actuators are commonly categorized into two main types: stack actuators and stripe actuators. Stack actuators consist of multiple layers of piezoelectric material stacked on top of each other, allowing for higher displacement and force output. On the other hand, stripe actuators have a single layer of piezoelectric material arranged in a stripe pattern, offering faster response times and higher frequency capabilities. Both types of actuators play a crucial role in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, medical devices, and more.</p></p>
