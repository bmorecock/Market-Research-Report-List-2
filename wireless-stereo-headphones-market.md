<p><p>Wireless stereo headphones come in two main market types: In-Ear and Over-Ear. In-Ear headphones are small and lightweight, fitting directly into the ear canal for portability and convenience. They are ideal for on-the-go use and are often used during workouts or commutes. Over-Ear headphones, on the other hand, have larger ear cups that rest over the ears, providing a more immersive sound experience and better noise isolation. They are popular for home or studio use where comfort and sound quality are priorities.</p></p>
