<p><p>Silica ore market types include Quartz, Tridymite, Cristobalite, and Coesite. Quartz, the most common type, is widely used in various industries for its thermal and mechanical properties. Tridymite is known for its high-temperature stability and wide application in the ceramics industry. Cristobalite is valued for its low thermal expansion and high chemical purity, making it ideal for use in the production of refractories. Coesite, a rare form of silica, is prized for its hardness and is used in the manufacturing of cutting tools and abrasives.</p></p>
