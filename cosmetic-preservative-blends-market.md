<p><p>Cosmetic preservative blends are categorized into different types based on their chemical composition and function. The main types include parabens, formaldehyde-releasing preservatives, halogenated preservatives, alcohols, organic acids, and others. Parabens are commonly used due to their effectiveness against a wide range of microbes. Formaldehyde releasers release small amounts of formaldehyde to prevent microbial growth. Halogenated preservatives contain halogen atoms and are chemically stable. Alcohols are effective at disinfecting and preserving cosmetics. Organic acids work by lowering the pH of products to inhibit microbial growth. Other preservatives include natural alternatives like essential oils.</p></p>
