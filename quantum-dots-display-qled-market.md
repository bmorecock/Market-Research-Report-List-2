<p><p>Quantum Dots Display (QLED) technology has two main types: Quantum Dot Enhancement Film (QDEF) and pure QLED. QDEF is a film layer that enhances the color and efficiency of traditional LED-backlit displays, while pure QLED uses quantum dots directly on the display panel for improved color reproduction and brightness. The QLED market is growing rapidly, with both QDEF and pure QLED technologies being adopted in various display applications such as TVs, monitors, and smartphones for their superior picture quality and energy efficiency.</p></p>
