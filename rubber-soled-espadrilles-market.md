<p><p>Rubber-soled espadrilles come in two main market types: natural rubber sole and synthetic rubber sole. Natural rubber soles are made from the sap of rubber trees and are known for their durability and flexibility. They are also environmentally friendly as they are biodegradable. Both types of rubber soles provide traction and comfort for wearers of espadrilles.</p></p>
