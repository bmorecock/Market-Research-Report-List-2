<p><ul><li>Natural Antioxidant</li><li>Synthesis Antioxidant</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Biodiesel antioxidants can be classified into two main types: natural antioxidants and synthetic antioxidants. Natural antioxidants are derived from plant-based sources and are considered more environmentally friendly. They include compounds such as tocopherols and phenolic compounds. On the other hand, synthetic antioxidants are chemically produced and often have a longer shelf life. These include BHA and BHT. Both types of antioxidants are used in biodiesel to prevent oxidation and degradation, ensuring the fuel remains stable and efficient.</p></p>
