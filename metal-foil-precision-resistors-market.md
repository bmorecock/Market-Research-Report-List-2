<p><p>Metal foil precision resistors are available in two main market types: SMD type and Through Hole type. SMD (surface mount device) type resistors are smaller in size and are designed for automated assembly processes. They are suitable for compact electronic devices. Through Hole type resistors are larger in size and are manually soldered onto a circuit board. They are commonly used in applications where durability and stability are key requirements. Both types of resistors provide high precision and accuracy in controlling electrical resistance.</p></p>
