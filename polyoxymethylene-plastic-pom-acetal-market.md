<p><p>Polyoxymethylene plastic, also known as POM or Acetal, is a versatile material used in various industries due to its high strength, stiffness, and chemical resistance. The two main types of POM are homopolymer and copolymer. Homopolymer POM is a material made from a single type of monomer, resulting in a higher level of crystallinity and improved mechanical properties. Copolymer POM, on the other hand, is a material made from a mixture of different monomers, offering enhanced thermal stability and lower levels of shrinkage during processing.</p></p>
