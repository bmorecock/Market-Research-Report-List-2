<p><p>The hot-melt PSA market can be categorized into resin type and rubber type. Resin type HMPSA typically uses synthetic resins such as acrylics, polyesters, and polyethylenes. These offer high versatility and adhesion properties. On the other hand, rubber type HMPSA utilizes natural rubber or synthetic rubber, known for their excellent tack and flexibility. Both types are widely used in industries such as packaging, construction, and automotive for their fast-setting and strong bonding capabilities.</p></p>
