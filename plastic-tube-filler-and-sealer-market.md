<p><p>Plastic tube filler and sealer machines come in two main types: automatic and semi-automatic. Automatic machines are fully automated, requiring minimal human intervention and capable of filling and sealing tubes at high speeds. On the other hand, semi-automatic machines require some level of manual operation, such as loading tubes onto the machine or removing finished products. While automatic machines are ideal for high-volume production environments, semi-automatic machines are better suited for small to medium-scale manufacturing operations.</p></p>
