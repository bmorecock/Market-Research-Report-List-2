<p><p>Optical fiber and plastic conduits are used for protecting and routing fiber optic cables in various applications. The market is divided into two main types: rigid conduits and flexible conduits. Rigid conduits are made of tough materials like PVC or metal and provide strong protection for the cables. Flexible conduits, on the other hand, are made of more pliable materials like plastic or corrugated tubing, allowing for easier installation in tight spaces. Both types play a critical role in ensuring the efficient transmission of data through fiber optic cables.</p></p>
