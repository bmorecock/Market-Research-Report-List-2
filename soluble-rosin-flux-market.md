<p><p>Soluble Rosin Flux is available in three main types - liquid, paste, and solid. Liquid flux is convenient for applications that require precise control and easy application. Paste flux has a thick consistency, making it ideal for applications that require a longer contact time. Solid flux is typically in the form of flux-cored solder wire, providing a convenient all-in-one solution for soldering. Each type offers unique benefits and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the soldering application.</p></p>
