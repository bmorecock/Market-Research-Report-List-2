<p><p>The lubricant additives market can be categorized into two types: single component and additive package. Single component additives are individual chemicals added to lubricants to improve specific properties, such as oxidation resistance or friction reduction. Additive packages, on the other hand, are combinations of multiple additives designed to enhance overall lubricant performance across a range of parameters. Both types play a crucial role in formulating high-quality lubricants that meet the diverse needs of various industries.</p></p>
