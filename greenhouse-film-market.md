<p><p>Greenhouse film market types include Ethylene Vinyl Acetate (EVA), Linear Low-density Polyethylene (LLDPE), and Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE). EVA films offer high light transmittance and UV resistance. LLDPE films are known for their toughness and durability, making them ideal for harsh environmental conditions. LDPE films provide good flexibility and low cost, making them suitable for short-term crop protection. Each type of greenhouse film caters to specific needs and preferences of farmers, ensuring optimal growth conditions for crops.</p></p>
