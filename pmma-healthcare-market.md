<p><p>PMMA healthcare market can be categorized into three main types: General PMMA, Heat Resistant PMMA, and Impact Resistant PMMA. General PMMA is widely used in various medical devices and equipment due to its versatility and transparency. Heat Resistant PMMA is specifically designed for applications requiring high temperature resistance, such as sterilization processes. Impact Resistant PMMA is engineered to withstand physical impact and is commonly used in medical devices that may undergo rough handling or potential drops. Each type serves a specific purpose in the healthcare industry.</p></p>
