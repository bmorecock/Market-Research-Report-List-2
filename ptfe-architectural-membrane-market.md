<p><p>PTFE Architectural Membrane market includes two main types: Hydrophobic PTFE Membrane and Hydrophilic PTFE Membrane. Hydrophobic PTFE Membrane repels water and is often used in outdoor structures to provide superior weather protection. On the other hand, Hydrophilic PTFE Membrane absorbs water and is ideal for applications where moisture management is crucial, such as in greenhouses or interior spaces. Both types offer unique properties that cater to different architectural needs and preferences.</p></p>
