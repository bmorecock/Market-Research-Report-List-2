<p><p>Agricultural detergents are classified into three main types based on their pH levels: acid detergent, neutral detergent, and alkaline detergent. Acid detergents are formulated with low pH levels to effectively remove mineral deposits and protein-based soils from agricultural equipment. Neutral detergents have a pH level around 7 and are gentle on surfaces while effectively removing organic materials. Alkaline detergents have a high pH level and are used for heavy-duty cleaning tasks to remove grease, oil, and other stubborn soils from agricultural equipment.</p></p>
