<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The PCB Cutting Tools Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Straight Type Drill</li><li>Undercut Drill</li><li>Inverse Drill</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PCB cutting tools are essential for precise and efficient drilling in the electronics industry. Straight type drills are commonly used for standard drill holes, while undercut drills are designed to create holes with a larger diameter at the top than at the bottom. Inverse drills work in the opposite way, creating holes with a larger diameter at the bottom. Other types of PCB cutting tools include routers, end mills, and diamond saws, each serving specific purposes in the PCB manufacturing process. These tools ensure accurate and clean cuts for high-quality electronic components.</p></p>
