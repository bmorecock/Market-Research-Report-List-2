<p><p>Biogas plant market types can be categorized into wet digestion and dry digestion systems. Wet digestion involves the breakdown of organic materials in a liquid medium, producing biogas as a byproduct. This method is commonly used for waste materials with high moisture content. On the other hand, dry digestion systems operate on solid waste materials, converting them into biogas through anaerobic digestion. Both wet and dry digestion technologies play a crucial role in sustainable waste management and renewable energy production.</p></p>
