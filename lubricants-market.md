<p><p>Lubricants market consists of various types including Mineral Oil, Synthetic Lubricants, Bio-Based, and Greases. Mineral oil lubricants are derived from crude oil and are commonly used due to their cost-effectiveness. Synthetic lubricants are man-made and offer superior performance under extreme conditions. Bio-based lubricants are derived from renewable sources, making them more environmentally friendly. Greases are semi-solid lubricants composed of a base oil thickened with a soap or other thickener, providing stickiness and staying power in industrial applications.</p></p>
