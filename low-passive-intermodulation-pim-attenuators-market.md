<p><p>Low Passive Intermodulation (PIM) Attenuators are passive components used to reduce passive intermodulation distortion in RF systems. PIM distortion can occur when two or more signals mix together in a non-linear device, creating unwanted interference and degrading the overall performance of the system. Low PIM attenuators help mitigate this distortion by providing high levels of attenuation with low intermodulation products.</p><p>The Low Passive Intermodulation (PIM) Attenuators Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.9% during the forecast period. The demand for improved signal quality and reliability in these applications is fueling the need for low PIM attenuators to minimize intermodulation distortion.</p><p>Latest trends in the Low Passive Intermodulation (PIM) Attenuators Market include the development of advanced materials and manufacturing techniques to improve attenuation levels and reduce PIM distortion further.
