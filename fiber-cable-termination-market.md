<p><p>The Fiber Cable Termination Market includes different types of products such as Fiber Optic Pigtails and Fanout Kits. Fiber optic pigtails are short optical fibers with connectors already attached at one end, typically used for splicing or connecting to other optical fibers. Fanout kits are used to manage and organize multiple fibers within a fiber optic cable by splitting them into individual pigtails. These products are essential for efficient and reliable fiber optic network installations and maintenance.</p></p>
