<p><p>UV filters for personal care products can be divided into two main types: mineral and organic. Mineral UV filters, such as zinc oxide and titanium dioxide, physically block and reflect UV rays from the skin. Organic UV filters, on the other hand, absorb UV rays and convert them into less harmful forms of energy. Both types of UV filters are commonly used in sunscreen lotions, creams, and other personal care products to protect the skin from sun damage and reduce the risk of skin cancer.</p></p>
