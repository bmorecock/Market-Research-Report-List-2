<p><p>Bowfishing bows are specifically designed for shooting fish with an arrow attached to a reel. There are three main types of bowfishing bows on the market: recurve bows, compound bows, and lever bows. Recurve bows are traditional in design and offer simplicity and reliability. Compound bows provide more power and accuracy through their pulley system. Lever bows are a hybrid between recurve and compound bows, offering a smooth draw and fast arrow speeds. Each type caters to different bowfishing preferences and skill levels.</p></p>
