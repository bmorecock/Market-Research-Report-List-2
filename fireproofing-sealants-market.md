<p><p>Fireproofing sealants are essential in preventing the spread of fires in buildings. Two common types of fireproofing sealants are elastomeric and intumescent. Elastomeric sealants are flexible and can withstand movement in the building structure, providing a fire-resistant barrier. Intumescent sealants expand when exposed to heat, forming a protective char layer that insulates the surfaces from flames. Both types are effective in enhancing the fire safety of buildings and are used in a variety of applications to ensure proper fire protection.</p></p>
