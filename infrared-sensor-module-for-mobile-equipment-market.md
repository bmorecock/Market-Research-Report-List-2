<p><p>In the Infrared Sensor Module for Mobile Equipment market, there are two main types of sensors: Uncooled Infrared (UIR) and Cooled Infrared (CIR) sensors. UIR sensors use materials that are sensitive to infrared radiation without the need for cooling, making them more cost-effective and suitable for applications where precision is not critical. On the other hand, CIR sensors rely on cooling technology to achieve higher sensitivity and accuracy, making them ideal for more demanding applications that require precise infrared detection.</p></p>
