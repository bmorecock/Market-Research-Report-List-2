<p><ul><li>Single-Spark</li><li>Multi-Spark</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car spark coil market includes two main types: single-spark and multi-spark systems. Single-spark coils generate a single spark per ignition event, while multi-spark coils produce multiple sparks for each ignition cycle. Single-spark systems are simpler and more cost-effective, but multi-spark systems offer improved combustion efficiency and performance. The choice between these two types depends on the specific requirements of the vehicle and the desired level of performance.</p></p>
