<p><p>TV display panels come in three main types: HD, FHD, and 4K. HD panels offer a resolution of 1280x720 pixels, providing clear images but not as detailed as FHD. FHD panels have a higher resolution of 1920x1080 pixels, delivering sharper and more crisp visuals. 4K panels, also known as Ultra HD, feature a resolution of 3840x2160 pixels, offering the highest level of detail and clarity. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and viewing experiences.</p></p>
