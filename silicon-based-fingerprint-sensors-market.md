<p><p>Silicon-based fingerprint sensors are widely used in the market in two main types: swipe sensors and area sensors. Swipe sensors require the user to swipe their finger across a small sensor area to capture the fingerprint image. On the other hand, area sensors allow for the entire fingerprint to be captured by placing the finger on a larger sensor area. Both types offer reliable and accurate fingerprint recognition capabilities, catering to different applications and preferences in the biometric security market.</p></p>
