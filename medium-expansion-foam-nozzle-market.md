<p><p>Medium expansion foam nozzles are available in two main types - flange type and thread type. Flange type nozzles are designed to be quickly attached to a hose with the help of a flange, making them easy to install and remove. Thread type nozzles, on the other hand, are screwed onto a hose for a secure and leak-free connection. Both types of nozzles are widely used in firefighting and industrial applications for generating medium expansion foam for fire suppression.</p></p>
