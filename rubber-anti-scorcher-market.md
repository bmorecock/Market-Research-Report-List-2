<p><p>The rubber anti-scorcher market consists of two main types; powder and granular. Powder anti-scorchers are finely ground particles that can be easily mixed into rubber compounds for effective heat protection. Granular anti-scorchers are larger particles that provide a slower release of heat protection over time. Both types of anti-scorchers are commonly used in the rubber industry to prevent premature vulcanization and ensure consistent product quality. Each type offers unique advantages depending on the specific application requirements.</p></p>
