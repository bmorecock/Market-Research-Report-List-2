<p><p>Nickel Boron Plating Market types are categorized based on the percentage of nickel phosphorus (Ni-P) content in the coating. High Ni-P plating contains a high concentration of nickel phosphorus, Medium Ni-P has a moderate concentration, and Low Ni-P has a lower concentration. The choice of market type depends on the specific application requirements, such as hardness, corrosion resistance, and lubricity. High Ni-P provides excellent wear resistance, while Low Ni-P offers improved solderability. Medium Ni-P strikes a balance between hardness and ductility.</p></p>
