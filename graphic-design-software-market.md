<p><ul><li>Pixel-based Image Editors</li><li>Vector-based Image Editors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Graphic design software market can be divided into two main types: pixel-based image editors and vector-based image editors. Pixel-based image editors, such as Adobe Photoshop, work by manipulating individual pixels to create images. They are best suited for editing photographs and creating digital artwork with rich detail. On the other hand, vector-based image editors, like Adobe Illustrator, use mathematical equations to create scalable graphics. They are ideal for creating logos, illustrations, and other designs that need to be resized without losing quality.</p></p>
