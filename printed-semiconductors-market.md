Inkjet printing is known for its high resolution and precision, while gravure printing provides excellent consistency and quality. Screen printing is often used for large-scale production, whereas flexography is ideal for flexible substrate applications. Other methods may include digital printing or offset lithography. Each printing technique offers unique advantages and is tailored to different market needs within the printed semiconductors industry.</p></p>
