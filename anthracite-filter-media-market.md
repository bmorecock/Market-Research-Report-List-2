<p><p>Anthracite filter media can be categorized into two main types: Refined Anthracite Filters and Normal Anthracite Filters. Refined anthracite filters undergo additional processing to remove impurities and enhance their filtration efficiency. They are typically used in water treatment plants and industrial applications where high filtration quality is required. On the other hand, Normal Anthracite Filters are less processed and are commonly used in residential water filtration systems and swimming pools. Both types of anthracite filters play a crucial role in removing solid particles and impurities from water.</p></p>
