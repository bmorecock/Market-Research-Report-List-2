<p><p>Radio frequency filters are essential components used in various communication devices to eliminate unwanted signals and noise. There are primarily three types of RF filters in the market: Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) filters, Bulk Acoustic Wave (BAW) filters, and other types. SAW filters are known for their high performance and low cost, making them ideal for consumer electronics. BAW filters offer superior performance in terms of wider frequency range and higher power handling capabilities. Other types of filters may include ceramic filters, dielectric filters, and cavity filters.</p></p>
