<p><p>Polymerases market is divided into two main types: DNA polymerases and RNA polymerases. They play a crucial role in ensuring accurate duplication of genetic material. RNA polymerases, on the other hand, are enzymes responsible for synthesizing RNA molecules from DNA templates. Both types of polymerases are essential components of the molecular machinery that governs genetic information flow in living organisms.</p></p>
