<p><p>Single-use bioprocess sensors such as Optical Dissolved Oxygen Sensors and PH Sensors are essential tools in bioprocessing applications. Optical Dissolved Oxygen Sensors provide accurate and real-time monitoring of oxygen levels in bioreactors, ensuring optimal conditions for cell growth. PH Sensors measure the acidity or alkalinity of the bioprocess medium, allowing for precise control of pH levels. These sensors are disposable, reducing the risk of cross-contamination and eliminating the need for cleaning and maintenance, making them cost-effective and convenient solutions for bioprocessing industries.</p></p>
