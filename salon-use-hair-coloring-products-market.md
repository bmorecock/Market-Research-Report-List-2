<p><p>Salon-use hair coloring products are divided into three main market types: temporary hair dye, semi-permanent hair dye, and permanent hair dye. Temporary hair dye provides temporary color that can be easily washed out, making it ideal for special occasions. Semi-permanent hair dye lasts longer but gradually fades with each wash. Permanent hair dye offers more long-lasting results, as it penetrates the hair shaft and cannot be washed out. Each type caters to different preferences and needs of salon clients looking to change or enhance their hair color.</p></p>
