<p><ul><li>Single Index Detection</li><li>Multi-indicator Detection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cholesterol meters are devices used to measure levels of cholesterol in the blood. The two main types of cholesterol meters are single index detection and multi-indicator detection. Single index detection meters measure only one type of cholesterol, such as LDL or HDL, while multi-indicator detection meters can measure multiple types of cholesterol simultaneously. Single index detection meters are simpler and more cost-effective, while multi-indicator detection meters provide a more comprehensive picture of a person's cholesterol levels.</p></p>
