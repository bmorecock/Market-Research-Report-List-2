<p><p>Smartphone display panels come in various types, including LCD, OLED, IPS-LCD, and AMOLED. Each type offers different benefits and features. LCD displays use a backlight to illuminate the screen, while OLED displays produce their own light, providing deeper blacks and more vibrant colors. IPS-LCD panels offer wider viewing angles and better color accuracy than traditional LCD screens. AMOLED panels combine the benefits of OLED technology with active matrix control, resulting in high contrast ratios and energy efficiency. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and usage scenarios.</p></p>
