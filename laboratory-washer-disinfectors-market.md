<p><p>Laboratory Washer Disinfectors market consists of two main types: Cabinet (Single Chamber) Machines and Continuous Process Machines. Cabinet machines are designed for small to medium-sized laboratories and offer a single chamber for washing and disinfecting. On the other hand, Continuous Process Machines are more suitable for high-volume laboratories as they allow for a continuous flow of equipment through the washing and disinfecting process. Each type caters to different laboratory needs and volumes, providing efficiency and effectiveness in cleaning and disinfection processes.</p></p>
