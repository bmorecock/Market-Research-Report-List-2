<p><p>Automotive HVAC heat exchangers play a crucial role in the heating and cooling systems of vehicles. There are three main types: condensers, evaporators, and heat cores. Condensers are responsible for cooling down hot refrigerant gas into a liquid form. Evaporators absorb heat from the cabin and are vital for cooling the air inside the vehicle. Heat cores are designed to warm up the air before it enters the cabin. Each type of heat exchanger serves a specific function in keeping the vehicle's interior temperature comfortable for passengers.</p></p>
