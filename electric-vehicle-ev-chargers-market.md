<p><p>Electric Vehicle (EV) Chargers Market consists of two main types, On-Board Chargers and Off-Board Chargers. On-Board Chargers are integrated within the vehicle and are used to connect to external power sources to charge the vehicle's battery. Off-Board Chargers, on the other hand, are external charging stations that are installed separately from the vehicle and are used to charge the vehicle's battery. Both types play a crucial role in the growing EV infrastructure by providing efficient and convenient charging solutions for electric vehicles.</p></p>
