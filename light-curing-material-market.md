<p><p>Light curing materials are categorized into three types based on their emulsification process. External emulsification type involves the formation of emulsions outside the main system. Non-ionic self-emulsifying type includes materials that can form emulsions on their own without the need for external agents. Ion-based self-emulsifying type utilizes ions to facilitate the emulsification process. Each type of market offers different benefits and applications in various industries such as healthcare, cosmetics, and food processing.</p></p>
