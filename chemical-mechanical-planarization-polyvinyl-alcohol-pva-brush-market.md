<p><p>Roll Shape and Sheet Shape are two types of Polyvinyl Alcohol (PVA) brushes used in Chemical Mechanical Planarization (CMP). Roll Shape PVA brushes are cylindrical in nature and are used for continuous polishing of large surfaces, while Sheet Shape PVA brushes are thin and flat, suitable for smaller and more delicate applications. Both types of PVA brushes are essential in the semiconductor industry for achieving uniform and precise polishing of wafers and other electronic components during the CMP process.</p></p>
