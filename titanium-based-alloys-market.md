<p><strong>The Titanium-based Alloys Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Alpha Titanium Alloy</li><li>Beta Titanium Alloy</li><li>Alpha + Beta Titanium Alloy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Titanium-based alloys can be classified into three main types: Alpha Titanium Alloy, Beta Titanium Alloy, and Alpha + Beta Titanium Alloy. Alpha Titanium Alloys have a hexagonal close-packed structure and offer high strength at moderate temperatures. Beta Titanium Alloys, on the other hand, have a body-centered cubic structure and provide excellent toughness and ductility. Alpha + Beta Titanium Alloys combine characteristics of both alpha and beta phases, offering a balance of strength, corrosion resistance, and weldability. These different types cater to various industrial applications based on their unique properties.</p></p>
