<p><p>Recycling equipment and machinery market types include baler presses, granulators, extruders, and agglomerators. Baler presses are used to compress and bind materials for easy transportation and handling. Granulators are machines that break down materials into smaller pieces for recycling. Extruders are used to shape and form recycled materials into new products. Agglomerators combine materials into larger granules for recycling. These different types of machinery play a crucial role in the recycling industry by processing and reusing materials efficiently.</p></p>
