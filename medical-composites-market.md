<p><p>Medical composites market consists of carbon, ceramic, and glass fiber composites. Ceramic composites provide excellent biocompatibility and wear resistance, making them ideal for dental applications. Glass fiber composites offer good impact strength and flexibility, making them suitable for braces and splints. Each type of composite material plays a crucial role in the medical industry by providing unique properties to meet the diverse needs of patients and healthcare professionals.</p></p>
