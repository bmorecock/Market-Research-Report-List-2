<p><ul><li>Hot Rolled Plain Strip</li><li>Cold Rolled Plain Strip</li><li>Hot Rolled High Quality Strip</li><li>Cold Rolled High Quality Strip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Strip steel market can be categorized into four main types: Hot Rolled Plain Strip, Cold Rolled Plain Strip, Hot Rolled High Quality Strip, and Cold Rolled High Quality Strip. Hot Rolled Plain Strip is produced by heating steel ingots and rolling them to the desired thickness. Cold Rolled Plain Strip is produced by further processing hot rolled strip through cold rolling mills. Hot Rolled High Quality Strip and Cold Rolled High Quality Strip are produced with tighter tolerances and higher surface finishes for specialized applications.</p></p>
