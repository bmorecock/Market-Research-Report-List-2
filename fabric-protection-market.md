<p><strong>The Fabric Protection Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Seals in the Fibers</li><li>Penetrates the Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fabric protection products can be categorized into two main types: seals in the fibers and penetrates the fiber. Seals in the fibers products form a protective layer on the surface of the fabric, creating a barrier that repels liquids and stains. On the other hand, penetrates the fiber products work by actually entering the fabric and bonding with the fibers to provide long-lasting protection from spills and dirt. Both types of fabric protection are effective in extending the life of textiles and keeping them looking fresh and clean.</p></p>
