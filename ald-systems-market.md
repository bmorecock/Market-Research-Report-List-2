<p><strong>The ALD Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PE-ALD</li><li>Thermal ALD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ALD Systems market can be categorized into two main types: PE-ALD and Thermal ALD. PE-ALD utilizes plasma-enhanced techniques to deposit thin films on substrates, offering higher film quality and improved step coverage. On the other hand, Thermal ALD relies on thermal energy to create a chemical reaction between gas phase precursors, resulting in conformal and high-quality films. Both techniques have their advantages and are suitable for different applications in the semiconductor, MEMS, and microelectronics industries.</p></p>
