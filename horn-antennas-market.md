<p><p>Horn antennas are classified into three main types based on the design of their waveguides. Twistable waveguide horn antennas have a flexible structure that allows for adjusting the radiation pattern. Seamless non-twistable horn antennas have a continuous waveguide structure, providing stable performance. Non-twistable horn antennas have a rigid waveguide design, offering a fixed radiation pattern. Each type caters to specific applications and requirements, with twistable waveguide antennas offering flexibility, seamless non-twistable antennas providing reliability, and non-twistable antennas offering a consistent performance.</p></p>
