<p><p>The fuel delivery system market consists of various types of components such as fuel pumps, injectors, pressure regulators, filters, rails, air control valves, and throttle position sensors. Fuel pumps are responsible for delivering fuel from the tank to the engine, while injectors control the amount of fuel entering the combustion chamber. Pressure regulators maintain optimal fuel pressure, filters remove impurities, rails distribute fuel to injectors, air control valves regulate air intake, and throttle position sensors monitor the position of the throttle valve.</p></p>
