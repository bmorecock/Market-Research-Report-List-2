<p><strong>The MgO Panel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thin (Thickness Below 8 mm)</li><li>Medium (Thickness 8-15 mm)</li><li>Thick (Thickness Above 15mm)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>MgO panels are classified into three types based on thickness - Thin (below 8 mm), Medium (8-15 mm), and Thick (above 15 mm). Thin panels are commonly used in interior wall and ceiling applications where space is limited. Medium panels are suitable for both interior and exterior applications, offering a balance between flexibility and strength. Thick panels are typically used for heavy-duty exterior cladding or structural applications where high levels of durability and strength are required.</p></p>
