<p><p>Dialysis concentrates and solutions market types include acid concentrates, bicarbonate concentrates, and modifiers for haemodialysis concentrates. Acid concentrates are used to lower the pH of the dialysate solution, whereas bicarbonate concentrates help to maintain proper pH balance. Modifiers for haemodialysis concentrates are used to adjust the concentration of electrolytes in the dialysate. These different types of concentrates play a crucial role in ensuring the effectiveness of the dialysis process and maintaining the overall health of patients undergoing dialysis treatment.</p></p>
