<p><ul><li>R-C Oscillator</li><li>L-C Oscillator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Audio frequency oscillators are electronic devices used to generate a continuous audio signal. There are two main types of audio frequency oscillators in the market - R-C oscillators and L-C oscillators. R-C oscillators use resistors and capacitors to create a stable frequency output, while L-C oscillators use inductors and capacitors for the same purpose. Both types are commonly used in audio applications such as musical instruments, audio testing equipment, and telecommunications. Their market demand is driven by the need for precise and reliable frequency generation in various audio devices.</p></p>
