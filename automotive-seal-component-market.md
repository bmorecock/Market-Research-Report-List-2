<p><p>The Automotive Seal Component market consists of three main types: EPDM, NR, and SBR. EPDM seals are known for their excellent resistance to weathering, ozone, and aging, making them popular in outdoor applications. NR seals are made from natural rubber and offer good flexibility and abrasion resistance. SBR seals are cost-effective options that provide good physical properties and abrasion resistance. Each type of seal has its own unique qualities that make it suitable for different automotive applications, depending on the specific requirements.</p></p>
