<p><strong>The Slitter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flat Slitting</li><li>Extrusion Slitting</li><li>Round Slitting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flat slitting, also known as straight or razor slitting, is a common method that involves using sharp blades to cut material into strips of a desired width. Extrusion slitting, on the other hand, is a process where material is stretched and cut simultaneously. Round slitting, also known as shear slitting, involves using circular blades to cut material into strips. Each type of slitting market caters to different materials and production requirements, offering unique benefits in terms of precision, speed, and efficiency.</p></p>
