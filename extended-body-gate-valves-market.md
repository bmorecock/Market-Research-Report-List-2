<p><p>Extended body gate valves are available in two main types - screw type and socket weld type. In screw type gate valves, the bonnet is attached to the body with screws, providing a secure closure. On the other hand, socket weld gate valves have a bonnet welded to the body, which enhances durability and strength. Both types are commonly used in industrial applications where high pressure and temperature resistance are required, offering reliable performance and effective fluid control.</p></p>
